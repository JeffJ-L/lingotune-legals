LingoTune Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2025

LingoTune respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you may provide when using our app. This privacy policy outlines what data we collect, how it is used, and how you can control it.

Information We Collect

  • Basic account info (e.g., name, email) only if you log in using third-party providers like Facebook or Google.
  • Language progress and preferences to enhance your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and personalize your experience
  • To save your language learning progress
  • To improve app performance and features

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except when legally required or to enable essential app features (e.g., login APIs).

Cookies

LingoTune may use standard cookies or similar tools to remember your settings and session across visits.

User Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the LingoTune app. Just go to your profile settings and tap “Delete My Account.” This will permanently remove your profile, preferences, and progress.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, contact us at lingotunedevs@gmail.com
